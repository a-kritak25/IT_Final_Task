About Haven Café

Our Story

Haven Café was founded in 2015 with a passion for coffee and community. Nestled in a historic building in the heart of Kathmandu, we blend rustic charm with modern comfort to create a warm, welcoming space. Our vision was to craft a sanctuary where locals and travelers alike can savor exceptional coffee and connect.

Our beans are ethically sourced from local Nepali farmers, roasted to perfection, and brewed with care, reflecting our commitment to quality and sustainability.

Our Mission

At Haven Café, we aim to elevate the coffee experience by combining artisanal craftsmanship with a deep respect for our community and environment. We strive to source organic, fair-trade ingredients and reduce our carbon footprint through eco-friendly practices.

Our Team

Our dedicated baristas and chefs are the heart of Haven Café. Trained in the art of coffee-making and culinary excellence, they bring passion and creativity to every cup and dish. Meet our head barista, Anisha, who has won regional coffee art competitions, and our chef, Raj, who infuses local flavors into our menu.

Community Involvement

We believe in giving back. Haven Café partners with local farmers to promote sustainable agriculture and hosts monthly workshops to educate the community about coffee culture. We also support local artists by showcasing their work in our café and organizing open mic nights.
